## If Hair Is Greasy

Use this routine:

- Clarifying shampoo first.
- Then moisturizing shampoo.

## If Hair Is Low Porosity

Use:

- Warm water
- Steam treatments
- Lightweight products
- Grease for sealing

## If Hair Is High Porosity

Use:

- Cold water
- Gentle shampoo
- Creams and oils
- Moisture sealing

## For Length Retention

Focus on:

- Protective styles
- Low manipulation
- Moisture
- Limited heat
