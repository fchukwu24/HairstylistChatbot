# Washing Routine

## General Wash Day Steps

Follow this routine for wash day:

1. Split hair into 4 sections.
2. Use clarifying shampoo first when needed.
3. Follow with a moisturizing shampoo.
4. Apply wash-out conditioner.
5. Detangle in sections.
6. Leave conditioner in while working through the hair.
7. Rinse thoroughly.

## Clarifying Shampoo

Use clarifying shampoo:

- About once a month, or
- Whenever the hair has a lot of grease, oils, or butters.

Clarifying shampoo should be the first wash before moisturizing shampoo.

## Greasy Hair Routine

If hair is greasy:

1. Use clarifying shampoo first.
2. Follow with moisturizing shampoo.
