# Protective Styling and Hair Retention

## Hair Retention Tips

To help retain hair length:

1. Protect the lengths of the hair.
2. Keep manipulation minimal.
3. Keep hair moisturized.
4. Avoid excessive heat styling.

## Braids and Extensions

For moisture while wearing braids or extensions, use:

- Conditioner
- Grease
- Mousse

## Additional Protective Styling Tips

Helpful practices include:

- Wash hair in twists when hair is in protective style.
- Detangle carefully.
- Practice low manipulation styling.
