# Hair Porosity Guide

## Types of Hair Porosity

Hair porosity types include:

- Low porosity
- Medium porosity
- High porosity

## Low Porosity Hair

Low porosity hair may need help allowing moisture to penetrate.

Recommended care:

- Use warm water.
- Use steam treatments to help moisture enter the hair.
- Use lightweight products.

Suggested products:

- Aussie Miracle Curls
- TGIN Rose Water Smoothing Leave-In Conditioner

## Medium Porosity Hair

Medium porosity hair usually holds moisture better than low or high porosity hair.

Recommended care:

- Use clarifying shampoo only if hair is greasy.
- Otherwise, use moisturizing shampoo.
- Deep condition regularly.
- Detangle in sections.
- Let deep conditioner sit for 5-10 minutes.
- Rinse and place hair in a bun afterward.

## High Porosity Hair

High porosity hair may lose moisture quickly and needs help retaining moisture.

Recommended care:

- Use cold water.
- Use gentle shampoos.
- Avoid heat during conditioning.
- Protect the ends of the hair.
- Seal moisture with oil.
- Use creams and oils for moisture retention.

Suggested products:

- Cantu Hydrating Cream Conditioner
- TGIN Triple Moisture Replenishing Conditioner
- Shea Moisture Curl & Shine Shampoo
- TGIN Hydrate & Repair Conditioner
