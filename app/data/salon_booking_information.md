# Salon Booking Information

This salon specializes in textured hair, natural hair care, protective styling, silk presses, braids, locs, and weave/wig installs.

## Business Hours

The salon is typically closed on Sundays.

The salon is open:
- Monday through Friday from 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM
- Saturday from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM

## Service Notes

- Different services take different amounts of time. Shorter services like trims may take 45 minutes, while longer protective styles like knotless braids or box braids may take several hours.

## Booking Notes

Appointment availability depends on:
- the salon's opening and closing hours,
- the requested service duration,
- the stylist's existing appointments,
- and whether the requested appointment can fit before closing time.
