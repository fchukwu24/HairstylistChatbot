# Salon Policies: EYEMEAN IT LLC
## Cancellation Policy

Clients should cancel or reschedule appointments at least 24-48 hours in advance.

## No-Show Policy

Late cancellations and no-shows may result in fees ranging from 50%-100% of the service cost.

## Arrival Policy

There is a strict 15-minute grace period.

Arriving later may result in:

- An abbreviated service, or
- A required reschedule.

## Patience Policy

Quality work takes time.

Clients are expected to arrive with patience and understanding.

## Salon Code of Conduct

Clients should maintain a family-friendly environment.

The following are not allowed:

- Cussing
- Vulgar language
- Profanity

## Respectful Boundaries

Clients should keep conversations professional.

Clients should not:

- Flirt with staff
- Ask stylists personal questions about their private lives
- Ask stylists personal questions about business affairs

## Substance Policy

No drugs or illegal substances are permitted on the premises.

## Stylist Property

Clients should not touch stylist belongings, tools, products, or personal items.
